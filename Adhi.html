About Optimus Prime

Optimus Prime is the courageous leader of the Autobots. He is known for his wisdom, honor, and strong belief in freedom for all sentient beings. Optimus transforms into a powerful truck and leads the Autobots in their battle against the Decepticons to protect both Cybertron and Earth.
