About Megatron

Megatron is the powerful and feared leader of the Decepticons. He believes that Cybertron should be ruled through strength and domination. Megatron is known for his intelligence, ambition, and ruthless leadership. His ultimate goal is to conquer Cybertron and expand Decepticon power across the universe.
