About Transformers

Transformers is a science fiction universe about intelligent robotic beings from the planet Cybertron. These robots can transform into vehicles, weapons, and other machines.

Two major leaders define the conflict in the Transformers universe: Optimus Prime, the noble leader of the Autobots, and Megatron, the powerful and ruthless leader of the Decepticons. Their battle represents the eternal struggle between freedom and domination across the galaxy.
